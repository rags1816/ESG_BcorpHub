About this readiness tool vs the official BIA

This tool covers 28 core BIA questions (max 186 pts). The official online BIA at bcorporation.net has up to 200+ questions — the extra questions are sector-specific and context-adaptive, generated dynamically by B Lab based on your sector, legal structure, and prior answers. They cannot be pre-loaded here.

The 14-point gap covers advanced practices: supply chain living wage (manufacturing), patient financial assistance (healthcare), specific ownership structures. Scoring 80+ here is a strong foundation — advanced questions typically add 5–20 pts by sector.

Start official BIA →

Governance

Recommendation: aim for 85+ on this tool before starting the official BIA. The buffer accounts for advanced questions you may not qualify for in your sector.

📖 User Guide — ESG & B Corp Suite

i-NoCarbon · Change Today for a Better Tomorrow

What This Tool Does

The ESG & B Corp Suite helps you measure your organisation's ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) performance, generate regulatory-ready reports and prepare for B Corp certification — all in one place.

It covers the full reporting journey: from a 21-question maturity assessment through to a Scope 1, 2 and 3 carbon footprint, an AI-assisted ESG report and an estimated B Impact Assessment score.

💡 Tip: The more data you enter, the more accurate your results. Using partial data is fine — the tool will flag where inputs are missing and note this in your report.

The 8 Modules — In Order

  1. Intake & Context — Set your company profile, jurisdictions and reporting frameworks. Optional but strongly recommended — configures all other modules.
  2. ESG Diagnostic — 21 questions across 6 dimensions. Takes ~8 minutes. Scores your maturity and feeds your B Corp estimate.
  3. ESG Dashboard — View your overall score, pillar breakdown and a target-driven action plan. Requires Diagnostic.
  4. Carbon Calculator — Enter your Scope 1, 2 and 3 data across all 15 GHG Protocol categories. Takes ~15 minutes.
  5. Carbon Dashboard — View your footprint, carbon intensity and cost-reduction recommendations. Requires Carbon Calculator.
  6. ESG Report — Generate a Draft Report (AI, 30 sec) or Audit-Ready Report (fill 21 qualitative questions). Requires Diagnostic + Carbon recommended.
  7. B Corp Readiness — See your estimated B Impact Assessment score, gap analysis and 12-month roadmap to 80 points.
  8. Downloads — Offline templates to share with your Finance, HR, Legal and Operations teams.

Demo Mode vs Your Own Data

When logged in as a demo user, you have two options via the Demo Guide drawer on the right:

  • ABC Limited (demo data) — Loads pre-set data for a fictional UK professional services company. Use this to explore all outputs before entering your own data.
  • My Company — Empty forms where you enter your own figures. Your data is saved separately from the demo data and persists in your browser.
⚠ Data quality matters. Results are only as accurate as the data you enter. Missing inputs default to 0 and may understate your footprint or score. The tool flags wherever defaults are used.

AI Report Generation

The ESG Report Generator has two modes:

  • Draft Report — AI generates a complete ESG disclosure in ~30 seconds from your Diagnostic and Carbon data. Where data is missing, it inserts [Data required] placeholders.
  • Audit-Ready Report — You complete 21 qualitative disclosure questions (pre-populated where possible from your Diagnostic). AI can draft each answer for you to review and edit, then generates the full report from your finalised answers.

AI generation requires the administrator to enable it. If it is currently disabled, you can still complete the Audit-Ready questionnaire manually.

Emission Factors & Sources

Every emission factor and cost benchmark is clearly sourced inline:

  • UK electricity and gas: DESNZ 2025 (gov.uk)
  • DEFRA conversion factors: DEFRA 2024 (gov.uk)
  • EU electricity: EEA 2024
  • US electricity: EPA eGRID 2024
  • India electricity: CEA 2024
  • Energy prices: Ofgem Q2 2026
  • Investments: PCAF 2024

The Carbon Dashboard includes a collapsible Sources & Methodology table with all factors, rates and links to published sources. Custom overrides are flagged with an OVERRIDE badge.

B Corp Certification

The B Corp Readiness screen provides an estimated B Impact Assessment (BIA) score based on your Diagnostic answers, Carbon data and supplementary B Corp-specific inputs. The minimum threshold for certification is 80 out of 200 points.

This is not the official BIA — complete that at bcorporation.net. Use this tool to understand your current position and identify the highest-value actions before starting the formal process.

Data Privacy

All data you enter is stored in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to a server unless you use the AI report generation feature (which calls the Anthropic API). No personal data is stored or shared by i-NoCarbon.

🔧 Admin Guide — ESG & B Corp Suite

i-NoCarbon · Administrator Reference

Logging In as Admin

On the login screen, use the Admin Access card (right side). Enter username admin and the password provided by i-NoCarbon. Admin mode shows an amber ADMIN badge in the top bar and unlocks the gear icon (⚙) for the Admin Panel.

AI Toggle — The Most Important Control

The AI toggle is OFF by default. When off, demo users see a friendly message explaining AI is disabled and are redirected to the manual Audit-Ready questionnaire.

To enable AI generation: open the Admin Panel (⚙ gear icon) → AI tab → toggle "AI Report Generation" ON. You must also have entered a valid Anthropic API key. The AI pill in the top bar will turn green when active.

⚠ Cost reminder: each AI report generation costs approximately £0.001–£0.015 depending on model. See the AI tab for model selection (Haiku = faster/cheaper, Sonnet = higher quality).

Running a Demo

  1. Log in as admin.
  2. Open Admin Panel → Demo tab → click "Auto-Fill ALL Screens" to load ABC Limited data across every module, or use the Demo Guide drawer to fill screen-by-screen.
  3. To show a client what the outputs look like, click "Run Full Demo" — this fills all data, runs the carbon calculation and opens the Carbon Dashboard automatically.
  4. Toggle Admin View → OFF (AI tab) to see exactly what a demo user sees — no amber annotations.
  5. When done, click "Reset ALL Data" to clear everything.

AI Draft per Question (Audit-Ready Mode)

When AI is ON and the Admin Panel → Q&A tab → "AI Draft answers" is ON, each of the 21 qualitative questions in the Audit-Ready Report shows an "✨ AI Draft" button. Clicking it generates a draft answer from the Diagnostic and Carbon data. The user can then edit the answer before including it in the report.

Updating Emission Factors (Maintenance)

Admin Panel → Maintenance tab. Edit any emission factor or cost benchmark. Each field shows the current value and a link to the published source. Changes are saved to localStorage and apply immediately across all screens. "Reset to Defaults" restores the hard-coded baseline values.

💡 Check DESNZ and Ofgem annually (usually Q2 each year) for updated factors. DEFRA publishes conversion factors each summer.
